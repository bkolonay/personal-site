Bio

Hello, and thank you for visiting my slice of the internet!

I grew up in Pittsburgh, PA and received a bachelor's from DePaul University in Chicago in 2010. Shortly after finishing school, I went full time with an internship in an automated testing role with Cision.

Tired of the frigid winters of Chicago, I accepted a remote position with Sabre Hospitality in 2011 and moved to Los Angeles, CA. While working remotely, I also developed and publicly released a HIPAA compliant SAAS for private care management practices.

After yearning for closer team interaction, I transitioned into a real-life QA position with Guidance Software in 2015. Unfortunately for me, just as I started receiving mentoring to move into a development role, I was laid off due to downsizing.

Within two weeks I accepted a QA role with CoStar and was promoted to Software Engineer in Test within three months. Since then I have been working towards a Master's in Software Engineering at Cal State Fullerton (finishing 6/2019) and developing an open source automated test results dashboard.
